Great question. There are a lot of answers.

Behold, a small sample of the strawberry themed items in my home. Gaze upon them and bask in their charm!

USE #3

SUBJECT OF CULINARY CHEMISTRY

Also known in layman's terms as "baking," culinary chemistry has few ingredients as delicious as strawberries! You can make all kinds of sweets such as pies, cakes, turnovers, and more! They're good covered in chocolate, they're good atop a stack of pancakes... There's just no way you can go wrong!

USE #4

SUBJECT OF PERSONAL BRANDING

Need something to base your entire identity, personality, and personal brand off of? Look no further! I've had friends, family, and even professors tell me that when they see strawberries, they think of me now. I've wormed my way into their psyche during their daily lives like a jingle for a flooring company.

USE #5

SUBJECT OF BASIC CONSUMPTION

Last but certainly not least, strawberries are an excellent source of fiber.
