History - Trivia - Uses

  • Strawberries are perrenials!

  • The "seeds" of strawberries are actually "achenes," and are the ovaries of the strawberry plant.

  • Strawberries are about 91% water. That's even more than you and I!

  • Achenes are technically the fruit of the strawberry flower, not the strawberry itself.

  • Strawberries names come from the word "strewn," in reference to how the plant's runners cause the plant to spread outwards and become "strewn" about!

  • On average, a strawberry will have about 200 achenes.

  • Practice care when growing strawberries, their runners have a tendency to spread out and strangle other plants!
