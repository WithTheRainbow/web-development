I know what you're thinking: "Who even is this girl?!"

I've played video games as long as I can remember, and I dream of one day combining my love of creative writing with my programming degree in order to create one myself. All in all, if you remember one thing about me, it should probably be that I love strawberries... because I really, really love strawberries.
